Juan Manuel Torres

Juan is CPC Strategy Senior Software Engineer. He has a Master of Science in Computer Science form San Diego State University. On his thesis he wrote about a model of group behavior patterned after social processes that can be used in action video games.

With over five years of experience in the technology field, Juan has developed different web applications and services using Object Oriented best practices and Design Patterns. He is also interested in infrastructure automation and third party service integrations.

In his free time Juan enjoys spending time with his wife, coding at the nearby coffee shop, hiking, and taking photographs.

You can follow him on Twitter @onema.
