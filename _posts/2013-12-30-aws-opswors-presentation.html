Automated Provisioning and Application Deployments Presentation - January 8 2014

OpsWorks is almost one year old and I'm proud to say that I have used it since day one! Before OpsWorks I understood the importance of automating processes such as launching new instances, deploying code and auto-scaling. I used a few different PaaS, including PagodaBox, AppFog, and AWS Elastic Beanstalk, but none of these quite cut it.

While AWS OpsWorks is not perfect by any means, it does enable developers to quickly solve some of the most common issues of managing the application life cycle.

If you are interested in hearing the presentation RSVP at Meetup.com, and the meeting will be held at the Invisible Children offices Downtown San Diego at 6:30 PM. There is usually free food and beer!
